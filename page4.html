About Me

Marie Raleigh is a counselor with further qualifications in relationship /couple therapy and psychosexual therapy.

She completed a two year Post Graduate Diploma In Relationship and Psychosexual Therapy in 2018. Marie is a qualified Relate Counsellor having worked with individuals and couples since 2010 . She has a private practice based at Health Plus GP surgery commencing July 2019.She is a member of the College of Sexual and Relationship Therapists (COSRT) and the British Association of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP).
