About Me

Marie Raleigh is a therapist with further qualifications in relationship /couple therapy and psychosexual therapy.

Marie completed a two year Post Graduate Diploma In Relationship and Psychosexual Therapy in 2018. She is a qualified Relate Counsellor having worked with individuals and couples since 2010 . She has a private practice based at Health Plus GP surgery. She is a member of the College of Sexual and Relationship Therapists (COSRT) and the British Association of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). 

Qualifications

Marie’s trainings and qualifications include:

●London Diploma for Psychosexual and Relationship Therapy
●Post Graduate Diploma Couple Therapy. University of Hull
●Advanced Counselling Diploma
●She is Registered Nurse (RGN ) midwife (nonpracticing) and SCPHN.
